Our Heritage

Woven in the
Desert Glow

Al-Masrah was founded with a singular vision: to honor the ancient craftsmanship of the Himalayas while embracing the majestic warmth of Middle Eastern traditions.

Our journey is a testament to the enduring beauty of the hand-crafted. Each fiber is treated with the reverence it deserves, bathed in the same golden light that has inspired poets and artisans for generations.

Today, we continue to bridge these worlds, creating pashminas that are more than just garments—they are wearable heirlooms that carry the warmth of the sun and the soul of the artisan.

Why Al-Masrah

Built on Principles That Never Fade

Every piece we create is guided by a commitment to the earth, the artisan, and the enduring beauty of heritage craftsmanship — values we have held since 1982.
